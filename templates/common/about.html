Our purpose

Pets are family. When life gets busy, you don't just need someone available - you need someone you can trust. Sittr makes it easy to compare caretakers by what matters most: services, pet types, location, price, and reviews.

Mission

To make finding a caretaker simple - with enough transparency that you feel confident leaving your best friend in good hands.

Motivation

Sittr was founded in 2023 after hearing the same story again and again: "I can find someone online, but I can't tell if they're right for my pet." Our goal is to remove that uncertainty and help you choose based on clear information and real feedback.
